QASPER is a public question-answering benchmark over NLP papers created by the Allen Institute for AI and collaborators. The upstream dataset is marked **CC BY 4.0**.

- Project/data page: <https://allenai.org/data/qasper>
- Dataset paper: *A Dataset of Information-Seeking Questions and Answers Anchored in Research Papers* (Dasigi et al., 2021)
- License: <https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/>

The QASPER license applies to the upstream QASPER material. It does **not** declare or imply a license for this repository as a whole.

Raw QASPER files and adapted paragraph text are stored under the Git-ignored `backend/data/public_evaluation/` cache. The repository contains only adapter code, a synthetic format fixture, attribution, and content-free aggregate/failure-ID reports.
